About Me

Hello World! My name is Seungjib Kim and I was born and raised in a small industrial city called Ansan in South Korea. I've been interested in art in various ways since I was young. I remember drawing a lot, filming silly short movies of me and my friends and carving soaps and erasers into little animal statues or toy cars. Although not many people would find my work impressive, I enjoyed doing it and that was enough for me.

I consider myself an optimistic, spontaneous but calm person who is inspired and often thrilled by creative ideas and projects, and I'm always enthusiastic about getting involved. I am motivated by the sense of accomplishment and pleasure I get when every aspect of my work is perfectly pleasing to my clients, colleagues and most importantly, myself, and I use that procedure as fuel for me to carry on. Staying creative is the most important point in developing my career and will be a pleasant yet challenging lifetime task.
